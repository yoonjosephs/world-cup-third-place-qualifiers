Position within each group is computed from points → goal difference → goals scored → fair-play (card) score → FIFA ranking. Head-to-head results between tied teams aren't modeled — if two teams in a group are tied on all five criteria, check the head-to-head result yourself.

Round of 32 — if the group stage ended right now

8 of the 12 group winners face a best third-placed team; the other 4 face a runner-up. Which third-place team lands where depends on which 8 groups supply a qualifying third-place finisher — FIFA fixed all 495 combinations in Annex C of the regulations, and that exact table drives the matchups below.
